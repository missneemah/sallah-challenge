Low and Slow

posted by BGB,November 15,2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food(usually vaccum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath is precisely set to the target temoerature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, cooking for Geeks, JefPotter describes it as " ultra-low-temperature poaching"

Location:
Bakers Corner, Seekonk, MA

Hours:
